Why TaskStation AI?

Most task managers just list your tasks, but humans need space to breathe, recharge, and focus. TaskStation AI intelligently spaces your daily tasks into “stations” with optimized breaks, personalized to your mood and energy level.

How It Works

  • Input your tasks: Add tasks with optional duration and priority.
  • Tell us your mood: Feeling tired or energetic? The AI adjusts your plan accordingly.
  • Smart Scheduling: Tasks become stations with purposeful breaks to prevent burnout.
  • Real-time Adaptations: Update your energy mid-day and get a refreshed plan.
  • End-of-day Summary: See your accomplishments and mood trends.
